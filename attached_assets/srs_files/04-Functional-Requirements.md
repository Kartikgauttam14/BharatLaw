# Functional Requirements

## FR-1 Document ingestion
System shall ingest HTML, PDF, and scanned PDFs.

## FR-2 Parsing
System shall parse text, headings, citations, and metadata.

## FR-3 OCR
System shall OCR scanned documents.

## FR-4 Deduplication
System shall identify duplicate or near-duplicate records.

## FR-5 Search
System shall support full-text and filtered search.

## FR-6 Browsing
System shall allow browse-by court, act, year, bench, judge, and topic.

## FR-7 Bookmarking
Users shall bookmark documents.

## FR-8 Download
Users shall download available public PDF copies where permitted.

## FR-9 AI summary
System shall summarize judgments in concise bullet form.

## FR-10 Admin
Admins shall view ingestion logs, failures, and source status.

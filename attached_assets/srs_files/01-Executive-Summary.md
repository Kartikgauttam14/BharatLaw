# Executive Summary

## Project Name
BharatLaw AI

## Goal
Build a scalable Indian legal research platform that provides:
- Supreme Court judgments
- High Court judgments
- Bare Acts
- Constitution and amendments
- Rules, regulations, notifications, and gazette material
- Fast legal search
- AI summaries and related-case discovery

## Business objective
Create a searchable, structured, and updateable legal database that can power:
- Android app
- Web app
- Public API
- Internal admin dashboard

## Key constraints
- Prefer official and public sources
- Respect robots.txt, terms of use, and copyright
- Keep ingestion auditable and reproducible
- Support English first, Hindi later

## Success criteria
- Search returns relevant cases in under 1 second
- Ingestion pipeline is idempotent
- New judgments can be added daily
- App can serve both full text and metadata

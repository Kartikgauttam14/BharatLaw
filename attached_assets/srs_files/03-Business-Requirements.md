# Business Requirements

## BR-1 Source aggregation
Aggregate judgments and legal texts from official/public sources.

## BR-2 Search
Provide court-wise, act-wise, section-wise, judge-wise, year-wise, and keyword search.

## BR-3 Legal metadata
Store robust metadata for every document.

## BR-4 AI features
Generate summaries, issue lists, and similar case suggestions.

## BR-5 Updates
Support automated refresh and change detection.

## BR-6 Multi-platform delivery
Expose data through web, Android, and API.

## BR-7 Admin control
Allow moderation, reindexing, and source health monitoring.

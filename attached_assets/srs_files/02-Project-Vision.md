# Project Vision

## Vision statement
Provide a single, modern legal intelligence platform for Indian law with trustworthy source tracking, powerful search, and AI-assisted research.

## Problems solved
- Fragmented legal sources across multiple portals
- Hard-to-search judgment text
- Poor metadata consistency
- Time-consuming case research
- Limited discoverability of related law

## Target users
- Students
- Advocates
- Legal researchers
- Startups
- Compliance teams
- General public

## Product pillars
1. Accuracy
2. Searchability
3. Source traceability
4. Freshness
5. Scalability
6. Compliance

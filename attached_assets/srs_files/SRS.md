# BharatLaw AI — Master SRS

This is the master specification for an Indian legal research platform.

## Scope
The platform aggregates and indexes:
- Supreme Court judgments
- High Court judgments
- Bare Acts
- Constitution and amendments
- Rules, notifications, and gazette material
